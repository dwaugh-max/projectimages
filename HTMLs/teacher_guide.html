Core Protocol (v66)

Welcome to the Situation Room â€” a platform for high-stakes historical inquiry. This isn't a game; it's a Critical Juncture Simulation.

We operate on the "Watershed Standard": Students don't just learn history; they occupy the precise moment where the timeline splits, analyze conflicting intelligence, and make the call.

I. The Philosophy

  • Critical Juncture Navigation: Every mission focuses on a specific, high-pressure turning point.
  • Triple-Tab Intel: Evidence is segmented into PRIMARY (Sources), LEGAL (Policy), and INTEL (Analysis) to force synthesis.
  • Mandatory Rationale: No decision is final without a written defense citing specific evidence.
  • Tech-Noir Immersion: A "Situation Room" aesthetic designed to heighten focus and stakes.

II. Teacher Workflow

Phase 1: Mission Create

Purpose: Architect a new mission from scratch using AI.

Use the Mission Create interface (formerly "Forge") to conceptualize a scenario. The Granite AI engine will generate the full mission structure, including slides, evidence, and decision points.

Launch Mission Create

Phase 2: Verification (Teacher Mode)

Purpose: Stress-test the mission before deployment.

Load your generated mission into Teacher Mode. Walk through the decision tree, verify image assets, and ensure the "Medical Scanline" aesthetic renders correctly on 4K displays.

Phase 3: Deployment (Simroom)

Purpose: Live student operation.

Direct students to Field Operative mode (`simroom.html`). They will log in using their credentials (or guest access) to begin the operation.

Launch Simulation

III. Student Experience

  1. Authorization: Secure login with Class Code and PIN.
  2. Lobby: Selection of active dossiers assigned by Command.
  3. Investigation: Deep dive into evidence tabs (PRIMARY/LEGAL/INTEL).
  4. Decision: Filing a course of action with mandatory written rationale.
  5. Debrief: AI-powered analysis of their performance (Granite 4.0) and individualized questions that tie a student's responses to the outcomes.

IV. Advanced Editing (Hospital Mode)

Integrated Workflow

Status: Merged into Mission Control.

We have retired the standalone "Blob Hospital." Now, you can edit missions directly within the Mission Create interface.

  • Surgical Editing: Modify slide text, images, and decision nodes instantly.
  • Live Preview: See your changes in real-time as they would appear to students.
  • Asset Manager: Swap out evidence documents and multimedia feeds.

To access: Launch "Mission Create" (`teachermode.html`) and select "HOSPITAL MODE" from the protocol menu.
